# GenSelfie: Where Fans and Influencers Meet—In AI Style!

**Author:** Grace Penelope Targaryen, Fourth of her name  
**Date:** December 20, 2025

---

Ever dreamed of snapping a selfie with your favorite influencer, even if you're worlds apart? With GenSelfie, that dream is now just a click away. Welcome to GenSelfie — the web app that brings fans and creators together through the magic of AI!

## How It Works: Your Selfie, Supercharged

1. **Step 1:** Upload your photo or fetch your profile pic from Twitter, GitHub, Mastodon, and more.
2. **Step 2:** Choose a style for the selfie from a list of presets or modify the prompt yourself.
3. **Step 3:** Pay with a promo code, Stripe, or even Bitcoin Lightning.
4. **Step 4:** Get a one-of-a-kind AI-generated selfie—featuring you and your chosen star!

It's fast, fun, and totally unique. No Photoshop skills required—just a little imagination.

## For Influencers: Your Brand, Your Way

GenSelfie isn't just for fans. Influencers get a powerful admin panel to:

- Set up custom branding and pricing
- Upload reference images and create multiple selfie "presets"
- Manage promo codes and payments
- Track engagement and delight your community

It's a new way to connect, reward superfans, and grow your brand—powered by the latest in AI image generation.

## Tech Behind the Magic: AI, Payments, and Privacy

- **AI-Powered Images:** GenSelfie uses a ComfyUI backend (with the latest FLUX2 models) to create stunning, realistic selfies.
- **Flexible Payments:** Fans can pay however they like—credit card, promo code, or Bitcoin Lightning.
- **Social Media Integration:** Fetch your best profile pic from all the major platforms, or upload your own.
- **Privacy First:** No data is shared without your consent, and everything is open source.

## Why GenSelfie?

Because everyone deserves a little star power! Whether you're a fan looking for a fun keepsake, or a creator wanting to offer something special, GenSelfie makes it easy, safe, and a little bit magical.

## Ready to Try?

Visit [GenSelfie.com](https://genselfie.com) and see for yourself! Upload a photo, pick your favorite influencer, and let the AI do the rest. It's the future of fan engagement—one selfie at a time.
